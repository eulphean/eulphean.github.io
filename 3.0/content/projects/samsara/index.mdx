Saṃsāra is a monumental video installation inspired by the nine Genesis paintings in the Sistine Chapel, featuring nine AI generated videos embedded inside a meticulously crafted architecture of sever machines disguised as Corinthian Columns. Developed with Unreal Engine, Blender, Stable Diffusion, and After Effects, the work introduces new pipelines and workflows at the intersection of Generative AI and real-time 3D rendering.
