The system was architected around a conversational AI pipeline that combines LLM reasoning with structured film metadata. The backend processes user prompts through a multi-stage pipeline: intent classification, preference extraction, candidate retrieval from the film database, and final ranking with explanation generation.

The frontend was built as a responsive Next.js application with real-time streaming responses, allowing users to see their recommendations materialize as the AI reasons through its selections.

<Result>

CineFives launched as a public web app in 2026, quickly gaining traction among film enthusiasts. The conversational approach to recommendations proved far more engaging than traditional list-based interfaces, with users averaging over 5 sessions per visit.

The AI pipeline consistently generates nuanced, surprising recommendations that go beyond mainstream picks, earning positive reception from both casual viewers and cinephiles.
